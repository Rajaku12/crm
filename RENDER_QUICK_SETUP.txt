WHERE TO UPDATE: Render Dashboard
================================================================================
1. Go to: https://dashboard.render.com
2. Login to your Render account
3. You'll see your services listed

================================================================================
BACKEND SERVICE - Environment Variables
================================================================================
STEP 1: Click on your Backend Service (e.g., "crm-11o1" or "zenith-crm-backend")
STEP 2: Click on "Environment" tab (at the top)
STEP 3: Click "Add Environment Variable" button
STEP 4: Add/Update these one by one:

ALLOWED_HOSTS
crm-11o1.onrender.com,localhost,127.0.0.1

CORS_ALLOWED_ORIGINS
https://your-frontend-url.onrender.com,http://localhost:5173,http://localhost:3000
(Replace 'your-frontend-url.onrender.com' with your actual frontend URL)

SECRET_KEY
[Generate a strong secret key - use Django's secret key generator]

DEBUG
False

DATABASE_URL
[Auto-set by Render - don't change]

================================================================================
FRONTEND SERVICE - Environment Variables
================================================================================
STEP 1: Go back to Dashboard, click on your Frontend Service
STEP 2: Click on "Environment" tab (at the top)
STEP 3: Click "Add Environment Variable" button
STEP 4: Add this:

VITE_API_BASE_URL
https://crm-11o1.onrender.com/api

================================================================================
AFTER UPDATING ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES
================================================================================
1. Backend Service → Manual Deploy → Deploy latest commit
2. Frontend Service → Manual Deploy → Deploy latest commit
3. Wait for both to finish deploying
4. Test: https://crm-11o1.onrender.com/api/ (should show JSON)
5. Test: Your frontend URL (should load without blank page)
